📚 QC Guidelines

Westgard Rules

  • 1₂s: One control exceeding 2SD
  • 1₃s: One control exceeding 3SD
  • 2₂s: Two consecutive exceeding 2SD
  • R₄s: Range between controls >4SD
  • 4₁s: Four consecutive >1SD
  • 10x: Ten consecutive on same side

Frequency

  • Daily: Before patient testing
  • After calibration
  • After maintenance
  • New reagent lot
  • Every 8 hours of operation

Actions

  • Failed QC: Reject patient results
  • Investigate cause
  • Document corrective action
  • Repeat QC before patient testing
  • Contact manufacturer if needed
